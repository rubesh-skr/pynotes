```python
# Program 6: Swap numbers
def main():
    a = 5
    b = 10
    
    print("Before swap:", a, b)
    
    temp = a
    a = b
    b = temp
    
    print("After swap:", a, b)

main()
```

    Before swap: 5 10
    After swap: 10 5
